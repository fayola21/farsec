Display should show a single line: &quot;AA BB.&quot;        Display shows two lines:    AA  BB. Note that the same fragment  without the &lt;p&gt; tag  will show correctly:       AA &lt;form style=&quot;display:inline&quot;&gt;BB&lt;/form&gt;.    Shows as &quot;AA BB.&quot; </pre>
