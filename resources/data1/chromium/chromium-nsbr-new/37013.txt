Click the titlebar        Nothing happens        The window is unmaximized.    Chrome enters move mode immediately after pressing down the mouse button.   This makes KWin unmaximize it. Native KWin windows don't enter move mode   until you keep the mouse button pressed for a few seconds (or you move the   mouse  obviously).
