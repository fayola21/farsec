<title>Issue 39066 -   chromium -    [Content Settings] The expires drop-down box option &quot;When I close my browser&quot; doesn't work in set cookie/data prompt - Project Hosting on Google Code</title> <pre>   1. Go to &quot;Content Settings&quot; -&gt; &quot;Cookies&quot;  2. Clear exceptions and saved cookies  3. Check the option &quot;Ask me when a site tries to set data&quot;  4. Access google.com  5. On pop-up set cookie/data prompt  check &quot;Ask me every time&quot; then click  on &quot;Show details&quot; and change the expires drop-down box to &quot;When I close my  browser&quot;  finally click on &quot;Allow&quot; button  6. Repeat steps 5 if other cookies prompt shows  7. Close chrome browser and then relaunch chrome  8. Go to &quot;Content Settings&quot; -&gt; &quot;Cookies&quot; -&gt; &quot;Show cookies and other site data&quot;      The saved cookies of google.com are still shown in the saved cookies list   and when check the cookie's information  the expires date of cookies is  still the original expiration date/time  not the date/time you close your  browser.       </pre>
