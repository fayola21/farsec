<title>Issue 26169 -   chromium -    We should not dispatch extension events unless somebody is listening - Project Hosting on Google Code</title> <pre> The extension system currently listens to all tab and window events and   builds up JSON details to send to extensions  even if no extensions are   listening or even installed.    This is pretty wasteful and we shouldn't do it.    As a side-effect  this will probably reduce the frequency of <a title="Crash in JSONWriter::WriteWithOptionalEscape on shutdown" class=closed_ref href="/p/chromium/issues/detail?id=25558"> bug 25558 </a>. </pre>
