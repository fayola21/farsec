TabbedPanel accepts only ITab(s)    TabbedPanel manages a List of ITab(s).This prevents instantiation of TabbedPanel(s) made of any custom ITab implementation.Every reference to List&lt;ITab&gt; should be converted to List&lt;? extends ITab&gt;.Alternatively  TabbedPanel should be declared as:public class TabbedPanel&lt;T extends ITab&gt; extends Panel{}depending on the preferred strategy.
