camel-spring is dependent on spring-aop    &lt;dependency&gt; &lt;groupId&gt;org.springframework&lt;/groupId&gt; &lt;artifactId&gt;spring-aop&lt;/artifactId&gt; &lt;version&gt;${spring-version}&lt;/version&gt; &lt;/dependency&gt;is needed in pom.xml for camel-spring as its needed to let camel-spring component work out-of-the-box. Without it the camel-example-spring does not work and blows up with a ClassNotFoundException when you use camel:run
