Impossible to use placeholders in endpointName and serviceName attributes of cxf:cxfEndpoint    Here is how endpointName and serviceName are declared in XSD.&lt;xsd:attribute name='endpointName' type='xsd:QName' /&gt; &lt;xsd:attribute name='serviceName' type='xsd:QName' /&gt; So the xml parser fails to parse the following element&lt;cxf:cxfEndpoint id='service' address='${http.service.endpoint.path}' wsdlURL='${http.service.wsdl.path}' serviceName='${http.service.serviceName}' endpointName='${http.service.portName}'&gt;&lt;/cxf:cxfEndpoint&gt;
